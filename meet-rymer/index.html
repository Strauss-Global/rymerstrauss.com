Rymer Strauss | Lemonade Mogul | LEGO Architect | Author

A Rising Entrepreneur

Ryme Strauss is not your typical entrepreneur. For that matter, Rymer is not your average grade schooler.

By day, Rymer is a student who loves to read, play soccer, practice judo, run cross country, swim, and spend time with his friends and family. He has a keen sense of adventure honed across vacations, forts, hideouts, and anywhere his imagination takes him.
Rymer is also interested in understanding what it means to be an entrepreneur. From coming up with business ideas, crafting plans, and forming budgets, Rymer loves learning about businessand he wants to share this passion with other kids his age. His first book, The Big Money Squeeze, is designed to spark entrepreneurial drive in kids across the country.

Rymer and his family live on Hilton Head Island in South Carolina.
